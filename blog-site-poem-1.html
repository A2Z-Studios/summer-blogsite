Spilled Ink Entry 1:

Sept-21-2015 Blaze

Heart in a Blaze

like a wildfire

raging through the veins

of her soul

Foggy smoke

trapped in her lungs

leaves her skin feeling frigid cold

Although her heart is burning

and her mind is racing

she’d dare let heat escape her lips

‘cause she knows the burning sun

after a bitter night

is fire disguised as a gleaming kiss

She would rather imitate the moon

modest in essence

and glow amongst the stars

Instead of chase after dawn

and face the truth that

we never really see purpose

in who we are

worse than having too many secrets is having none at all .24
